Mr. Hernandez Pedraza is a computational policy analyst of considerable range. He holds dual degrees in Economics and Anthropology from the Universidad de los Andes (2020) and is presently completing a Master of Science in Computational Analysis & Public Policy at the University of Chicago’s Harris School (expected June 2026).

His coursework includes Machine Learning, Neural Networks, NLP, GPT/BERT, LLM Agents, Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Decision Modelling. He serves as Teaching Assistant for Python Programming and the Data Science Clinic.
